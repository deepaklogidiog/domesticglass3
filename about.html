About Us

Building Better
Spaces Through Glass

Domestic Glass has been helping homeowners, architects, interior designers, and businesses realise their vision through thoughtfully engineered glass solutions since 1991.

Our Story

Founded in 1991. Still building every day.

We combine craftsmanship, technology, and execution expertise to deliver projects that are visually stunning and built to last. From a single fabrication unit to a full-service glass solutions company, our growth has been driven by the trust our clients place in us.

Today, Domestic Glass operates with an in-house fabrication facility, dedicated project management teams, and precision installation specialists, all working together to deliver seamless glass solutions across India.
